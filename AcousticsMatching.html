Acoustics Matching

This page gives some demo audio examples for Acoustics Matching in reverberant environments. Each file consists of a concatenation of three audio signals. The first part is the input signal. The second part is the reference to which the input must be matched. The third part is the processed input.

The listener is expected to answer the following questions. To do so, please provide a score in the range of (0 - 10) for each of the following questions.

  • Question 1:Rate the quality of the match between part 1 and part 2.
  • Question 2: Rate the quality of the match between part 2 and part 3.
  • Question 3: Rate the quality of part 3 for distortions and abnormalities.
